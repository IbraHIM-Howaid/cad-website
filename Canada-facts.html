CANADA: SIZE, DIMENSION & POPULATION

Canada is 5,400 kilometres east to west, with six time zones 4,600 kilometres north to south 9,984,670 square kilometers 33,850,000 people (2010 projection)



Canada comprises about 6.7% of the world’s land area yet has:
  • 20 % of the world’s remaining wilderness
  • 25 % of the world’s wetlands
  • 10 % of the world’s forests
